Week 5 News

Weaver Warriors take down the Orienta Orioles 39-26.

The Orienta Orioles were missing the reigning MVP Aidan Roy in this game, and it showed. They struggled to generate offense, although Nate Bard had a solid performance with twelve points and 9 rebounds. Julian Epstein played a great game, but unfortunately it was cut short with 3 minutes left to play as he injured his ankle. It looks like he will be out for a few weeks. We all wish him a speedy recovery.

Village Vipers beat the Manor Mavericks 41-33.

This was a statement performance from the Vipers. After starting the season with two losses, the Vipers are now 3-2 and have just beaten the only undefeated team in the LBL.
